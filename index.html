About Jacob

Photography for
restaurants worth
remembering.

Jacob Sawyer is a hospitality photographer based in the North West of England. Working with independent restaurants, cafes, and bars, he specialises in capturing the atmosphere that keeps guests coming back — the light, the craft, the character.

Every venue has a story. Jacob's job is to tell it — honestly, atmospherically, and in a way that makes someone reach for a reservation.
